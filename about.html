About the House

Built in Sumilere,
worn across Lagos.

Excel Ventures started from a simple irritation: most "luxury" menswear sold to Nigerian men was loud by default — heavy logos, bright colour blocking, more decoration than design.

We build the opposite. A dark, disciplined palette of cognac brown and sky blue, cut lines that respect the body, and fabric choices that hold up to Lagos heat without losing their shape by evening.

Every piece is designed and fitted from our base on Dunojaiye Street, Sumilere — close enough to the men we dress to adjust a sleeve the same week we cut it.

How we fit a piece

Process

Conversation

We start by listening to how you actually move through your week — what you're dressing for, not just what you like the look of.

Measure

A full set of measurements taken in person at Sumilere, recorded against your name for every future order.

Cut & Construct

Pattern drafted to your frame, cut by hand, and assembled by our tailoring team over one to two weeks.

Fit & Finish

A final fitting before the piece leaves the house — adjusted on the spot until the line sits right.

Dress for the room you're walking into, not the one you left.
